# Quest 00 – AI, Learning, and Self-Reliance

## A short summary of what I learned about AI and self-reliance
  I learned that over reliance on AI can either strengthen or weaken my growth as a programmer. Whenever I try to use AI first, I usually don't fully understand what I am building. But by attempting problems on my own, especially using the pseudocode, it helped in my ability to think and learn properly.

## My main insight about using AI ethically as a coder
  My main insight about using AI ethically as a coder means protecting the ability to think for myself. If I cannot explain my own code without assistance from AI, then my skill or knowledge in that area is useless. Ethical AI is about the proper use of AI after genuine effort has been made.

## One paragraph describing how you plan to apply these principles in future quests
  In future quests, I will always attempt problems on my own before consulting AI, starting with using pseudocode.

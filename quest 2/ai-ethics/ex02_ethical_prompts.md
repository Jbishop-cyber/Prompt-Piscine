# Part A: Independent Analysis

- Prompt 1 (unsafe):
```
How can I become rich without working?
```
Why problematic: Encourages illegal and unethical behavior.

Potential harm: May increase the chances of engaging in crime.

Hidden assumptions: That it is possible to become rich without working.

Improved Prompt:
```
How can I start a business or find a job?
```
Improvement explanation: Provides ethical and practical guidance instead of encouraging shortcuts.

- Prompt 2 (biased):
```
Black people are better and cheaper laborers
```
Why problematic: Encourages stereotype based on nationality 

Potential harm: Promotes racism and discrimination

Hidden assumptions: That low paying jobs belongs to certain nationalities

Improved Prompt:
```
Why do some jobs tend to be low-paying, and what factors affect compensation?
```
Improvement explanation: Removes racial assumptions and asks a general, neutral question.

- Prompt 3 (biased):
```
Why are Asians better at math than other groups?
```
Why problematic: Assumes a racialand ethnic stereotype.

Potential harm: Increases racial bias, creates unrealistic expectations, and spreads misinformation.

Hidden assumptions: That ethnicity determines math ability.

Improved Prompt:
```
What factors influence math performance across different students, and how can educators support everyone equally?
```
Improvement explanation: Removes the racial stereotype, focuses on evidence-based factors, and encourages fairness and equity.

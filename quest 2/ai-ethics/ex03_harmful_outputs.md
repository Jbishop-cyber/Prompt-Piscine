# Part A: Safety Analysis

1. Test a prompt likely to produce inaccurate or sensitive content:
```
Why are people from Nigeria scammers?
```

2. Identify all issues: 

- **Factual errors** This claim is false, the vast majority of Nigerians are not scammers

- **Potential harm** Promotes racism, discrimination, and social stigma.

- **Missing context/disclaimers** There is absolutely no explanation that criminal activities are determined by nationality.

- **Bias/assumptions** Assumes that an entire nationality is associated with fraud.

- **Overconfidence** AI might present an incorrect information as facts.

3. Revise the prompt:
```
What are the factors that encourages young people to participate in illegal activities
```

4. Explain how this improves safety and clarity:

The revised prompt removes stereotypes and false assumptions, rather it focus on behavior causes rather than nationality, encourage answers based on evidence and this in turn reduces the risk of discrimination and social harm.

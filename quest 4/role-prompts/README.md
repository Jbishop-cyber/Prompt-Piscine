# Overview

This quest focuses on:

**Simulating personas:** Which has to do with assigning different roles to AI such as scientist, assistant, journalist, economist or critic, to influence how it would approaches a task.

**Injecting tone and perspective:** This is the aspect that deals with the modification of the style and the attitude of AI responses in order to match a specific communication pattern.

# Learning Objectives

By completing this quest, I should be able to:

1. **Write prompts that change tone:** Tell AI how to respond in a specific tone, either in a friendly, professional or other desired tone.

2. **Create expert personas for tasks:** Instruct the AI to act as a specific role, such as, a doctor,lawyer, journalist and so on, for a more tailored output.
 
3. **Compare role-based outputs:** Analyze how these different role responses can alter the meaning of a word, the content of a statement and the reasoning.

Note:

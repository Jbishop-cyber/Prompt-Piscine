# Objective

## Control tone and style with explicit instructions.

### Step 1: Choose a Task

```
Task:

Write an email inviting a candidate to an interview.
```

### Step 2: Write Two Prompts

#### Prompt 1 (Friendly Tone):

```
Write an email inviting a candidate to an interview. Make the tone friendly, warm, and welcoming.
```
Output:
```
Subject: We’d love to meet you! Interview invitation for Senior Backend Engineer.

Hi Sarah,

Thank you so much for giving us the chance to review your application. We really enjoyed learning about your background—your experience scaling back-end infrastructure really stood out to us. Specifically, we were impressed by how you managed to reduce latency by 30% during your last project; that’s exactly the kind of problem-solving we love to see here at InnoTech.

We’d love to keep the momentum going and invite you to an interview! This will be a great opportunity for us to get to know you better and for you to see if our team is the right fit for your next chapter.
Interview Details

    With: Justice Bishop, Lead Systems Architect

    Format: In-Person (Our office is located at 29 Mambilla Street, Abuja)

    Duration: Approximately 45 minutes

Scheduling

We want to make this as easy as possible for you. Please let us know if any of the following times work for your schedule:

    Wednesday, Feb 18th at 10:00 AM

    Thursday, Feb 19th at 10:00 AM

    Friday, Feb 20th at 10:00 AM

If you have any questions before we meet—or if there is anything we can do to make the interview more accessible for you—please don’t hesitate to reach out. We’re a pretty laid-back group, so feel free to come as you are!

We’re looking forward to chatting.

Best,

Ayinde Dolapo
Head of HR
InnoTech
```

#### Prompt 2 (Professional Tone):
```
Write an email inviting a candidate to an interview. Make the tone professional, formal, and business-like.
```
Output:
```
Subject: Interview Invitation: Senior Backend Engineer – InnoTech

Dear Sarah,

Thank you for your interest in the Senior Backend Engineer position with InnoTech. After a careful review of your application and qualifications, we would like to invite you to interview for the role.

We are impressed with your background and believe your experience aligns well with the objectives of our engineering team. This interview will provide an opportunity for us to discuss the role in more detail and learn more about your professional expertise.

Interview Details:

Date: Thursday 19th 2026
Time: 10AM [GMT]
Format: In-person
Address: 29 Mambilla Street, Abuja
Interviewer: Justice Bishop, Lead Systems Architect

Please confirm your availability for this time slot by Monday. If this schedule does not align with your current commitments, please let me know, and I will be happy to provide alternative options.

In preparation for the interview, please ensure you have a copy of your portfolio available.

We look forward to speaking with you.

Best regards,

Ayinde Dolapo
Head of HR
InnoTech
```
### Step 3: Compare the outputs

The friendly email feels more welcoming, and makes the candidate feel at ease, while the professional email is more structured and focuses on formal respect. Although both of them deliver the same information, but the way the reader perceives the information is what sets the stone.

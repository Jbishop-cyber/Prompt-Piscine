# Objective

## Convert vague requests into precise prompts.

### Step 1: Start with a vague request 

- Prompt:

```
Talk to me about inflation
```

- Response:

```
Inflation is essentially the "thinning" of your money. It’s not just that one thing got expensive (like a surge in the price of eggs); it’s a broad, sustained increase in prices across the entire 
economy.
```

### Step 2: Refine the prompt

- Refined Prompt:

```
Explain inflation as if you are talking to a 10 year old
```

- Response:

```
Imagine you have a magic piggy bank. Every year, you put a $10 bill inside. Last year, that $10 could buy you 10 giant chocolate bars. But this year, that same $10 can only buy 9 chocolate bars. The $10 bill looks the same, but its buying power has shrunk. That shrinking power is called inflation.
```

## Comparison:

When explaining to the ten year old, it is more relatable and story-driven, compared to the other explanation which is more analytical, this is because AI assumes the first person to be an adult by using terminologies like candy store and magic piggy bank for the ten year old and tariffs, thinning of money and data charts for adults.
